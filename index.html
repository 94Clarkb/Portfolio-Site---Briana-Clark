My Work

"Hakunetsu Studios" - Responsive Web-App

This web-app is designed to be a merchandise e-commerce shop for those looking for things related to Hakunetsu Studio's universe of books, webtoons, anime, video games and merchandise. Anyone and everyone can find something that fits their taste.




"Kanbanza" - Native Mobile App

This app is an essential tool for anyone who values organization and efficiency, allowing users to manage tasks at all levels and track their progress seamlessly. With a contemporary and minimalist interface, it also features collaboration tools that enable teams to work together effectively and complete projects promptly.




"Cooking Confidence" - Responsive Web App

This app will provide an easy to understand and easy to use resource for parents and caregivers alike to research, save and make delicious eats for their children and families overall. It will provide knowledge to allow users to learn new things about various topics related to feeding their loved ones. This will be the all-in-one place to go for all things food for families.




Productivity App - Mobile App

This app is designed for anyone needing to track tasks and projects, offering a timeline view that helps users see upcoming due dates, overdue tasks, and their overall schedule. Its friendly interface, complete with progress wheels and collaborative features, encourages users to stay on track and feel confident in achieving their goals.
