A Little About Me

I am a UI/UX Designer with a diverse background spanning Psychology, Healthcare, Education, and Retail. Each of these fields has contributed invaluable experience to my skill set.

UI/UX design enables me to serve others while pursuing my passion for creativity. My aspiration is to develop solutions that foster comfort, inspiration, and a sense of “coziness” in daily life, as I believe that life should not be overwhelmingly stressful all the time.

Now onto some fun questions!

  • Where are you based?

    I was born and raised, and still currently reside in Virginia, United States. I call myself a hermit because I’ve hardly been out of the state, but have managed to move 12 times within it.

  • How do you spend your free time?

    Outside of time spent with my toddler and husband, I enjoy some solitude time filled with crafty things. I enjoy crochet, drawing, writing stories, cross stitching and embroidery. I love using my hands, and they get kind of cramped if I’m not using them in some way. I’d love to get back into music someday soon, when my toddler doesn’t want to steal everything I hold. I also enjoy taking nature walks and playing the occasional video game. Cozy ones, of course.

  • What’s a fun fact about you?

    I’m not sure if it’s “fun”, but I completed my first novel at the age of 13, and am continuing to expand upon it today, nearly 17 years later! It is truly a labor of love. Fun fact about my fun fact is that I am now taking the novel and making it into a webtoon, as I think it fits in that medium better.
